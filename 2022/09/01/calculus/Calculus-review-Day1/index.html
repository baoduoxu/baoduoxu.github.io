Posted on 2022-09-01 Edited on 2022-09-02 In Math , Calculus Valine:
Symbols count in article: 3.1k Reading time ≈ 3 mins.

1：计算 \[ \int_0^{+\infty}\frac{x}{\mathrm{e}^x+1}\mathrm{d}x. \]

SOL1 \[ \begin{aligned}\int_0^{+\infty}\frac{x}{\mathrm{e}^x+1}\mathrm{d}x&=\int_0^{+\infty}\frac{-x\mathrm{d}(\mathrm{e}^{-x})}{1+\mathrm{e}^{-x}}\\&\xlongequal{\mathrm{e}^{-x}\mapsto x}\int_1^0\frac{\ln x}{1+x}\mathrm{d}x\\&=\int_0^1\frac{\ln(1+x)}{x}\mathrm{d}x \quad \text{Integration by parts}\\&=\int_0^1\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{n-1}x^{n-1}}{n}\mathrm{d}x\\&=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n}\int_0^1x^{n-1}\mathrm{d}x\\&=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n^2}\\&=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n^2}-2\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{(2n)^2}\\&=\frac{\pi^2}{12}.\end{aligned} \] 2：设 \[ f(a)=\int_1^{+\infty}\frac{\arctan ax}{x^2\sqrt{x^2-1}}\mathrm{d}x,a\ge 0. \] 求 \(f(a).\)

SOL \[ \begin{aligned}f'(a)&=\int_1^{+\infty}\frac{1}{x(a^2x^2+1)\sqrt{x^2-1}}\mathrm{d}x\\&\xlongequal{x\mapsto\sec x}\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\frac{1}{(a^2\sec^2 x+1)\sec x\tan x}\mathrm{d}(\sec x)\\&=\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\frac{1}{a^2\sec^2 x+1}\mathrm{d}x\\&\xlongequal{x\mapsto \tan x}\int_0^{+\infty}\frac{1}{(a^2x^2+a^2+1)(x^2+1)}\mathrm{d}x\\&=\int_0^{+\infty}\left(\frac{1}{x^2+1}-\frac{1}{x^2+\frac{a^2+1}{a^2}}\right)\mathrm{d}x\\&=\frac{\pi}{2}\left(1-\frac{a}{\sqrt{a^2+1}}\right)\end{aligned} \] 于是 \[ f(a)=\frac{\pi}{2}a-\pi\sqrt{1+a^2}+C. \] 而 \(f(0)=0,\) 因此 \[ f(a)=f(a)=\frac{\pi}{2}a-\pi\sqrt{1+a^2}+\pi. \] \(\square\)

3：设 \(f(x)\) 在 \([-1,2]\) 上三阶可导，且 \(f(-1)=1,f(0)=4,\) \(f'(-1)=8,f'(1)=24.\) 求证：存在 \(\xi\in[-1,2]\) 使得 \(f'''(\xi)=24\xi+30.\)

PROOF 考虑用多项式拟合 \(f(x).\) 利用待定系数法可得满足 \(p(-1)=1,p(0)=4,\) \(p'(-1)=8,p'(1)=24\) 的次数最小的多项式为 \(p(x)=x^4+5x^3+2x^2+x+4,\) 且 \(p'''(x)=24x+30.\) 那么我们只需证明 \(g(x)=f(x)-p(x)\) 的三阶导数存在零点即可。且 \(g(-1)=g(0)=g'(-1)=g'(1)=0,\) 则由Rolle定理得存在 \(\xi_1\in(-1,0)\) 使得 \(g'(\xi_1)=0;\) 存在 \(\xi_2\in(-1,\xi_1)\) 使得 \(g''(\xi_2)=0;\) 存在 \(\xi_3\in(\xi_1,1)\) 使得 \(g''(\xi_3)=0;\) 于是存在 \(\xi\in(\xi_2,\xi_3)\subset[-1,2]\) 使得 \(g'''(\xi)=f'''(\xi)-24\xi-30=0,\) 这就是要证的。\(\square\)

4：证明级数 \[ \sum_{n=1}^\infty \ln\left(1+\frac{1}{n}\right)\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{2n}\right)\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{2n+1}\right), \] 收敛并求其值。

SOL 显然 \(n\to \infty\) 时 \[ \ln\left(1+\frac{1}{n}\right)\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{2n}\right)\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{2n+1}\right)\sim \frac{1}{n^3}, \] 这表明其收敛。

记 \(a_n=\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{n}\right),\) 且注意到 \(a_{2n+1}+a_{2n}=a_n,\) 于是 \[ \begin{aligned}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}a_na_{2n}a_{2n+1}&=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(a_{2n}+a_{2n+1})a_{2n}a_{2n+1}\\&=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(a_{2n}^2a_{2n+1}+a_{2n}a^2_{2n+1})\\&=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{3}(a^3_{2n}+3a_{2n}^2a_{2n+1}+3a_{2n}a^2_{2n+1}+a_{2n+1}^3)-\frac{1}{3}(a_{2n}^3+a_{2n+1}^3)\\&=\frac{1}{3}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(a_n^3-a_{2n}^3-a_{2n+1}^3)\end{aligned} \] 而 \[ \sum_{n=1}^N(a_n^3-a_{2n}^3-a_{2n+1}^3)=a_1^3-\sum_{n=N+1}^{2N+1}a^3_n \] 且 \(N\to \infty\) 时， \[ 0<\sum_{n=N+1}^{2N+1}a^3_n< (N+1)\ln^3\left(1+\frac{1}{N+1}\right)<\frac{1}{(N+1)^2}\to 0 \] 于是 \[ \frac{1}{3}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(a_n^3-a_{2n}^3-a_{2n+1}^3)=\frac{1}{3}a_1^3=\frac{\ln^32}{3}. \] \(\square\)
